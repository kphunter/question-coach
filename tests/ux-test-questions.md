# UX Test Questions

A reference set of questions for manual UX testing of the Question Coach app.
Use these as sample input during Stage 2 (Produce Questions) to exercise the
full QFT workflow — classification, prioritisation, and discussion.

Questions are labelled **[O]** (open) or **[C]** (closed) to help verify that
the Stage 3 categorisation UI correctly handles both types.

---

## Questions

1. **[C]** Was the Berlin Wall torn down in 1989?
2. **[O]** How did the construction of the Berlin Wall affect everyday life for families divided between East and West?
3. **[C]** How many bones are in the adult human body?
4. **[O]** Why do some languages disappear while others spread to become global lingua francas?
5. **[C]** Did Marie Curie win more than one Nobel Prize?
6. **[O]** In what ways has social media changed how political movements organise and recruit?
7. **[C]** Is the Great Wall of China visible from space with the naked eye?
8. **[O]** What factors allowed the Roman Empire to sustain itself for so long, and what ultimately caused its collapse?
9. **[C]** In what year did women gain the right to vote in the United States?
10. **[O]** How might widespread automation reshape the relationship between work and personal identity?
11. **[C]** Does the human body contain more bacterial cells than human cells?
12. **[O]** What role did propaganda play in shaping civilian support during the Second World War?
13. **[C]** Who wrote *Crime and Punishment*?
14. **[O]** How does growing up bilingual affect cognitive development in children?
15. **[C]** Is Antarctica classified as a desert?
16. **[O]** Why do some scientific discoveries take decades to be accepted by the broader research community?
17. **[C]** What is the chemical symbol for gold?
18. **[O]** How has the globalisation of food supply chains affected small-scale farmers in developing countries?
19. **[C]** Was Nikola Tesla born before Thomas Edison?
20. **[O]** In what ways might rising sea levels reshape patterns of human migration over the next century?
