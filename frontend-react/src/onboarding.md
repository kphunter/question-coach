## Welcome to Question Coach

This tool guides you through the **Question Formulation Technique (QFT)** — a structured process for producing, improving, and prioritising your own research questions.

Your chat history is recorded anonymously to help improve the coaching experience. No personal information is collected or stored.

By continuing, you agree to this use of your session data.

---

## Moving between stages

The **Next stage** button in the bottom-right corner advances you through the six stages of the QFT process.

Each stage builds on the last — work through them in order. You can also step back using the **Back** button if you need to revisit earlier work.

---

## Tracking your progress

The **numbered circles** in the top-right corner show which stage you are currently on and which stages you have completed.

Completed stages are highlighted so you can see how far you have come at a glance.
