## Welcome to Question Coach

This tool guides you through the **Question Formulation Technique (QFT)**, a structured process for producing, improving, and prioritising your own research questions. See the Knowledge Base link at the top for more information.

Your chat history is recorded anonymously to help improve the coaching experience. No personal information is collected or stored.

By continuing, you agree to this use of your session data.

---

## Moving between stages

Use the **Next stage** button in the bottom-right corner to move to the next stage.

You can also go back using the **Back** button if you need to revisit earlier work.

---

## Tracking your progress

The **numbered circles** in the top-right corner show which stage you are currently on and which stages you have completed.
