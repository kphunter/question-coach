A six-stage framework developed by [[Dan Rothstein]] and [[Luz Santana]] at the [Right Question Institute](https://rightquestion.org/what-is-the-qft/) that facilitates learning how to ask better research questions through brainstorming, reflection, and refinement.

1. Question Focus
2. Produce Questions
3. Improve Questions
4. Prioritize
5. Discuss
6. Reflect
